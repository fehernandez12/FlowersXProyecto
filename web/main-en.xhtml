About us

Created in 2005 in Facatativá town, Santa Marta Flowers S.A.S. is a company dedicated to selling and exporting Colombian flowers to big businesses in countries such as the United States, Canada, and many others. Our mission is to be one of the leaders in the Colombian flowers market, considering that this is a national symbol and an offering from Colombia to the whole world.

Our portfolio includes orchids, roses, carnations and a lot of ornamental flowers species, be it in arrangements or standalone. These flowers are carefully selected and transported to ensure the best quality, and to fill your life with color and happiness.

Our products

Roses

Roses are ancient symbols of love and beauty. These flowers were sacred for a considerable number of ancient goddesses, and are used as a synbol or chastity and a lot of things alike. Roses are so important for our culture, that some words such as 'red' derive from their name in many languages.

Sunflowers

Much appreciated because of their beautiful yellow color and their wonderful properties, sunflowers also are exquisite ornamental plants and their bright color is a beautiful sight in any place or situation.

Orchids

Orchids have been known and appreciated by humans ever since the ancient ages. There are chinese scriptures depicting orchid plantations. In ancient Greece, they were praised for their medical and esoterical properties.
